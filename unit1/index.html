Summary


You are a ghost hunter that will go through different houses to seek the paranormal. The first house you will search is Tanglewood 6 Drive to discover what ghost generated for that game.

Game Content


There many different items and ghosts for you to explore along with that there are cursed items that help you locate the ghost room and get information about the ghost.

You must keep an close eye out for cursed items


Items to watch out for

  1. Tarot Cards(these are my favorite one)
  2. Monkeys Paw
  3. Music Box
